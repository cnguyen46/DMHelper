Rogue

Rogue is the master of stealth, agility, and precision, making them deadly adversaries both in and out of combat. They excel at navigating the shadows, striking swiftly when enemies least expect it. With their keen senses and quick reflexes, Rogues is the master of disarming traps, picking locks, and uncovering secrets.

Ideal for players who enjoy outsmarting their foes, taking calculated risks, and thriving in situations that require cunning and adaptability, Rogues are versatile characters who can excel in various roles, from infiltration and espionage to delivering lethal strikes in battle. Rogue uses Dexterity as their primary ability.

Skills Overview

Stealth: The Rogue can hide during the combat, and set up the perfect moment to strike enemys.

Backstab: A signature move that allows the Rogue to deal devastating damage when attacking from behind, catching enemies off guard.

Poison Dagger: Throw a poison blade from a distance, inflicting poison damage over time and weakening enemies.
