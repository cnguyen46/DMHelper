Warrior

Powerful and resilient frontline fighter, armed with heavy armor and a mighty weapon, Warrior is known for their strength and endurance. They thrive in close combat, able to withstand heavy hits while dishing out devastating blows. Perfect for those who prefer to charge into battle and protect their allies.

Warrior relies on their exceptional Strength and Constitution, allowing them to dominate the battlefield with raw power and unwavering resilience.

Abilities Value
Strength (STR) 17
Dexterity (DEX) 13
Constitution (CON) 15
Intelligence (INT) 10
Wisdom (WIS) 12
Charisma (CHA) 8

Skills Overview

Power Strike: The Warrior deals immense massive damage into a single enemy. The strike has a small chance to stun the target.

Taunt: forces enemies to focus their attention on them, drawing their aggression away from allies and ensuring the Warrior becomes the target of enemy attacks.

Defense Formation: The Warrior adopts a defensive stance, strengthening their armor and bolstering their defenses, reducing incoming damage and protecting allies in the area.
