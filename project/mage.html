Mage

Wielder of powerful arcane magic, capable of casting devastating spells, Mage relies on their intellect and magical knowledge to control the battlefield, casting fireballs, ice storms, and other elemental spells. While physically frail, their ability to manipulate magic makes them formidable foes from afar. Perfect for those who want to bend the elements to their will.

Mage uses Intelligence for casting the spell.

Abilities Value

Skills Overview

Firebolt: Launch a firebolt at enemies. It inflicts fire damage and leaves the burning area on the ground. If it hits target, it leaves the burn status for 2 turns.

Ice Barrier: A protective shield of frost that absorbs incoming damage and slows down enemies who dare to strike. Perfect for defense while preparing powerful spells.

Arcane Blast: A burst of pure magical energy that strikes with force, dealing high arcane damage. Especially effective against enemies with low magical resistance.
