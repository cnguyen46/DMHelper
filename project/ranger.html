Ranger

Master of ranged combat, skilled with a bow and arrow, with unmatched precision, Ranger can strike from a distance with deadly accuracy. Their agility allows them to stay out of harm's way while picking off enemies one by one. Ideal for those who favor finesse and strategy over brute force.

Ranger excels in Dexterity , making them highly effective in both close-quarters combat and ranged attacks. However, they thrive when engaging enemies from a distance, where their precision and agility truly shine.
