Cleric

Cleric is a divine spellcaster who draws power from the gods to heal the wounded, protect allies, and smite enemies. As a versatile and devout servant of their chosen deity, Clerics balance between healing support and offensive magic, making them an essential asset to any adventuring party.

Cleric's strength lies in their Wisdom , which fuels their spellcasting and supports other party member.
