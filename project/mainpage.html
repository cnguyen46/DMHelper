To begin a Dungeons & Dragons (D&D) game, you will need at least four players ready to embark on an epic adventure. As the Dungeon Master (DM), it is your responsibility to guide the players through the journey and set the stage for their quest. One of your first tasks is to input each player's chosen class, which determines their role, strengths, and abilities throughout the game. Players can explore detailed information about each class by referring to the class guidebook, where they will learn about the unique skills, talents, and ability points associated with their choice. This knowledge will help them make informed decisions and prepare for the challenges that await, ensuring an exciting and immersive experience for everyone involved.
